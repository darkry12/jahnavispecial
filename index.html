Okay. I'm going to be real for a second, which is uncomfortable for me so bear with it.

You're one of the most annoying people I've ever been friends with. You take forever to get ready, you have strong opinions about everything except what to eat for dinner, and you will send me a 7-minute voice note when a single text would have been fine.

And yet.

You're also the person I want to tell things to first. The one who laughs at the same dumb stuff I do. The one who shows up — actually shows up — when it matters. Not with advice, not with a solution, just with presence. That's rarer than you think.

I've watched you grow into someone who knows who she is and doesn't apologise for it. That's not nothing. That's actually really hard. You've done it in a way that's somehow still warm and funny and completely yourself.

I don't say this kind of thing often. Mostly because I'd never let you live it down. But you deserve to hear it in full, somewhere, at least once:

You matter. A lot. Specifically to me.

Thanks for being my person. You absolute idiot.
