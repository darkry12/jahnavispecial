Hey you,

Thank you for being there.

Thank you for staying — through the good phases and the bad ones. Through the days I was a lot, and the days I wasn't enough.

I know I wasn't always the best friend you deserved.
I know I made mistakes.
I know there were moments I could have done better.

But even after all of that — somehow you stayed.

Thank you for every conversation that went way too long.
Thank you for every laugh that didn't make sense to anyone else.
Thank you for every roast that I deserved.
Thank you for every random memory that I still think about.

And thank you for being one of the few people who made life genuinely better, simply by being in it.

No matter where life takes us next, I genuinely hope you stay exactly the way you are.

A little dramatic.
A little annoying.
A little sleepy.
A little idiot.

But completely unforgettable.

With love (and a lot of roasting), Your annoying best friend ♡
